How to reach?

The venue is located approximately 6 km from the city center. There is a high frequency of buses to the venue. You can take bus numbers 1, 2, 3, 5, and 8 from the city center bus stop (Kaupungintalo P).

People who are coming from airport can take bus number 8 to reach to city center. If the arrival time is after 00:30 then the only optimal option would be taking a Taxi from airport. Taxi cost approximately 30 to 40 euro from airport to city center.
